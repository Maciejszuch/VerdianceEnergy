Our Process

Click to discover our process below:

Step 1: Initial Consultation and Assessment
We start by scheduling a friendly consultation to understand your unique energy needs. Our team will conduct a comprehensive site assessment to identify opportunities for improvement, ensuring that we have a clear picture of how to best serve you.
Step 2: Customised Proposal
Based on our assessment, we’ll provide you with a detailed, personalised proposal. This plan will outline tailored energy solutions, estimated savings, and a clear path forward, so you know exactly what to expect.
Step 3: Project Approval and Planning
Once you’re ready to move forward, we’ll work with you to finalise the proposal. We’ll create a detailed project plan that respects your schedule and preferences, ensuring a smooth process from start to finish.
Step 4: Installation and Testing
Our experienced team will carry out the installation of your energy solutions with care and professionalism. We’ll conduct thorough testing to ensure everything is working optimally, so you can enjoy peace of mind knowing your investment is in good hands.
Step 5: Training, Support, and Monitoring
After installation, we provide comprehensive training on your new systems. Our commitment doesn’t end there—we offer ongoing support and regular monitoring to maximise your energy performance and savings, ensuring you always have a partner in your sustainability journey.
