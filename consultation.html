Consultation Services

Personalised Insights for a Greener Future

Our consultation service offers tailored, in-depth guidance to help you make impactful decisions about energy efficiency and sustainability in your home. Beginning with a comprehensive assessment of your property and specific goals, we identify key areas where energy improvements can make a meaningful difference. You’ll receive customised recommendations on energy-efficient technologies, sustainable materials, and upgrades that align with both your environmental objectives and budget. Whether you aim to cut energy costs, reduce your carbon footprint, or enhance your home’s overall efficiency, our team will support you every step of the way, delivering actionable insights and a clear, personalised path forward.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our consultation starts with an initial assessment of your property and a discussion about your sustainability goals. We then create a personalised report with recommendations for energy improvements tailored to your needs and budget.

The initial consultation typically lasts 1-2 hours, depending on the scope of your goals and property size. After this session, we provide a detailed report within a few days, which we’ll review with you to discuss next steps.
