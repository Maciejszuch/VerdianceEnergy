Who We Are

At Verdiance Energy, we are passionate about transforming homes into energy-efficient, sustainable, and future-ready spaces. Founded by a team of building services engineering experts, we bring together extensive technical knowledge, a commitment to environmental sustainability, and tailored energy solutions to meet the specific needs of each home.

We are a one-stop shop for all things energy efficiency—whether you need a simple consultation or a full home energy upgrade, we can accommodate any need or requirement. From assessing your current energy usage to implementing cutting-edge solutions, Verdiance Energy has you covered at every step of the journey.

Our Mission

We believe that energy efficiency is key to a greener, more sustainable future. Our mission is to make this future accessible to everyone by offering comprehensive services, from expert consultations to complete energy-saving solutions and smart home technology integration. Whether you're looking to lower your energy bills, improve comfort, or reduce your carbon footprint, we’re here to help make your home more efficient, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly.

Why Choose Verdiance Energy?

  • Expert-Led Approach: Backed by our qualifications as Low Carbon Consultants and Domestic Energy Assessors, we provide trusted, professional advice tailored to your home’s needs.
  • One-Stop Shop: We offer end-to-end solutions, from initial energy assessments to sourcing materials and overseeing full upgrades, ensuring that we can accommodate any need or requirement.
  • Sustainability at Heart: We work with trusted suppliers who provide materials that are as sustainable as possible, including FSC-certified wood and eco-friendly products. Our commitment to sustainability ensures your home is improved with minimal environmental impact.
  • Smart Home Integration: We embrace the future of home technology, ensuring your home not only runs efficiently but is also equipped with the latest in smart energy management.
